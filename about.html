About Waste Pollution

Waste pollution refers to the environmental damage caused by improper disposal of waste materials. Plastic waste, industrial waste, household garbage, and electronic waste affect the environment and create serious health problems.

Rapid urbanization and the increasing use of plastic products have greatly increased pollution levels. Waste thrown into rivers, forests, and open spaces harms wildlife and destroys natural ecosystems.

Proper waste management systems such as recycling, composting, and safe disposal methods are important to reduce pollution and protect the environment for future generations.
